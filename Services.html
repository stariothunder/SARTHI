Our Services

AI Helper offers a range of services designed to enhance the lives of disabled individuals, including the blind and amputees. Our commitment to accessibility and empowerment drives our service offerings.

Services for Accessibility

We have developed specialized features in our app to provide accessibility solutions:

  • Voice-Guided Navigation: Our app offers turn-by-turn voice guidance, making it easier for blind individuals to navigate unfamiliar places with confidence.
  • Text-to-Speech Integration: With text-to-speech capabilities, users can have written content read aloud, promoting accessibility for visually impaired users.
  • Sensory Feedback: We utilize advanced sensory feedback to provide tactile information, aiding blind individuals in perceiving their surroundings.
  • Intuitive Control: Our app enables users to control their prosthetic limbs with natural and intuitive movements, enhancing mobility and functionality.
  • Adaptive Learning: Through adaptive learning algorithms, our app assists users in mastering precise limb control over time.
  • Remote Support: Our support system includes remote troubleshooting and updates to ensure the best possible experience for users.
