About Us

At AI Helper, we are driven by a mission to make technology accessible and empowering for everyone. Our team is dedicated to creating innovative solutions that improve the lives of disabled individuals, including those who are blind and amputees.

Our Vision

We believe that technology has the power to break down barriers and transform lives. Our vision is to create an inclusive world where disability is not a limitation but a unique aspect of each individual.

How We Help

Through our cutting-edge app, we aim to provide assistance and support to those who need it most:

  • Accessibility for the Blind: Our app includes voice-guided navigation, text-to-speech capabilities, and advanced sensory feedback to empower blind individuals in their daily lives.
  • Enhanced Mobility for Amputees: We've developed state-of-the-art prosthetic limb control features, making it easier for amputees to regain control and lead active lives.

Join Us in Making a Difference

We invite you to be a part of our journey in creating a more inclusive society. Together, we can harness the potential of technology to empower and uplift those with disabilities. Join us as we continue to innovate and make a meaningful impact.
